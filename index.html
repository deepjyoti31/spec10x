Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the spec generation? expand_more
Our models are fine-tuned on thousands of engineering specs. While we recommend a human review, users report 90% accuracy in capturing intent and requirements.
Is my data secure? expand_more
Absolutely. We are SOC2 Type II compliant and encrypt all data at rest and in transit. We do not train our public models on your proprietary data.
Does it integrate with Linear? expand_more
Yes. Spec10x has a native 2-way sync with Linear, Jira, and GitHub Issues. Changes in specs automatically update ticket descriptions.
What data sources can I connect? expand_more
Interviews (upload), Zendesk, Intercom, Mixpanel, Amplitude, PostHog, HubSpot, Salesforce, Hotjar, FullStory, NPS surveys, and more via API.
Can I export tasks to coding agents? expand_more
Yes — Spec10x generates structured, atomic task definitions with context bundles (supporting quotes, design refs, acceptance criteria) compatible with Cursor, Claude Code, and Devin.
How does the feedback loop work? expand_more
After shipping, Spec10x tracks adoption and satisfaction metrics. It auto-connects outcomes to original user signals and generates impact summaries for your team.
Can my whole team use it? expand_more
Absolutely — comments, @mentions, spec review workflows, role-based access, and shareable roadmaps and reports for stakeholders.
